About SpotLit

SpotLit is a music suggester web application that runs on the Spotify API using Spotipy Python library and hopes to follow the Developer Terms and Conditions. SpotLit acts as the recommendator for Tracks, Albums, Artists and Playlists for people to share and discover new forms and variety of music each day. How we recommend music is done by our algorithm, although its not perfect right now, we hope to improve as time goes on.
